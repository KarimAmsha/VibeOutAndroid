# Privacy Policy — VibeOut (طلعة)

**Last updated:** July 1, 2026

This policy explains how the **VibeOut (طلعة)** app collects, uses and protects
your data. By using the app you agree to this policy.

**Developer:** Karim Amsha
**Contact:** karim.amsha@gmail.com

---

## 1. Data we collect

We only collect what the app needs to work:

**a. Account data (provided by you):**
- Email and password (managed and encrypted by Firebase Authentication).
- Name, display name and an optional bio.
- The city you select.
- Optional languages and interests.
- Avatar: we store only a **text URL** to a ready-made avatar — we do **not**
  upload any photos from your device.

**b. Content you create:**
- Outings ("vibes") you create or join.
- Chat messages inside outings.
- Reports you submit about users or content.

**c. What we do NOT collect:**
- We do **not** collect your precise (GPS) location. The app relies only on the
  **city you manually select**.
- We do not upload photos from your device.
- We use no advertising networks and never sell your data.

---

## 2. How we use your data

- To create your account and sign you in.
- To show places and outings relevant to your city.
- To let you communicate with other outing participants.
- To keep the community safe (reviewing reports, blocking).

---

## 3. Data sharing

- **With other users:** your display name, avatar, the outings you create and
  your in-outing messages are visible to the relevant participants. **Your email
  and password are never shown to any other user.**
- **With our service provider (Google/Firebase):** we use Firebase services
  (Authentication, Cloud Firestore) by Google to store data and run the app.
  This data is subject to Google's privacy policy.
- **Map data:** maps are shown via OpenStreetMap. We do not share your personal
  data with them.
- We do not sell your data or share it for advertising.

---

## 4. Deleting your data

- You can **delete your entire account** at any time from **Settings → Delete
  account**. This deletes your profile and associated data.
- For data requests or manual deletion, email **karim.amsha@gmail.com**.

---

## 5. Data security

We rely on Google/Firebase secure infrastructure. Data is transmitted over an
encrypted connection (HTTPS), and passwords are encrypted and not visible to us.

---

## 6. Children's privacy

The app is intended for people **18 years and older**. We do not knowingly
collect data from children.

---

We may update this policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an
updated "Last updated" date.
